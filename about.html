WHAT IS CYBER MARIO RUNNER?

Cyber Mario Runner is a free browser-based endless runner game inspired by the classic Chrome offline dinosaur game — but fully reimagined in a neon cyberpunk world.

Instead of a dinosaur hopping over cacti, you control a cyberpunk-armored Mario sprinting through a glowing neon city, leaping over illuminated pipe obstacles in four vivid color variants.

No download needed. No account required. Just open the page and start running.

GAME DETAILS

ENGINE

Pure HTML5 Canvas. No frameworks, no libraries. Runs at 60fps in any modern browser.

CONTROLS

SPACE / Arrow Up to jump. Arrow Down to duck. Click or tap also works. Double jump enabled.

SCORING

+1 point per frame survived. +100 points per gem collected. Speed increases over time.

LIVES

3 lives per run. Hitting a pipe costs one life. Ads show between lives to keep the game free.

NIGHT MODE

After 700 points the city switches to full night mode — darker sky, brighter neon glow.

HIGH SCORE

Your best score is saved locally in your browser via localStorage. No account needed.

HOW ADS WORK

Cyber Mario Runner is completely free to play. We show ads when you lose a life or when the game ends to keep the game available at no cost. Ads are served by Adsterra.

You can skip the ad after a short countdown. Clicking the ad supports the game's development and hosting. Thank you for playing!
