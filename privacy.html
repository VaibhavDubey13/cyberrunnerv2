OVERVIEW

Cyber Mario Runner ("the Game") is a free browser-based game. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play.

DATA WE COLLECT

We collect minimal data:

  • Your high score is stored locally in your browser's localStorage only — it never leaves your device.
  • We do not collect names, emails, or personal information.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking.

ADVERTISING

This game is supported by ads served by Adsterra. Adsterra may use cookies and tracking technologies to serve relevant advertisements. By using the game, you consent to Adsterra's data practices.

You can learn more at: adsterra.com/privacy-policy

THIRD PARTY SERVICES

  • Google Fonts — for typography (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Adsterra — for advertising

YOUR RIGHTS

Since we don't collect personal data, there is nothing to delete or export. You can clear your local game data (high score) by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site.
